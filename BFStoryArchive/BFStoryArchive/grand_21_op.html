The Bariura Empire, a military regime located in central Grand Gaia.The Empire was both an absolute monarchy and a great military power. It dominated numerous vassal states for many years, and enjoyed boundless prosperity.


However, the Empire would ultimately incite the anger of the gods through research it had been conducting in secret.And in doing so, they would set off the great war between humanity and the gods...


As it is widely believed to have caused the great war, the Bariura Empire was subject to a particularly intense offensive by the God Army.The Empire's dependent states began to collapse one after the other. With the Imperial capital completely surrounded by the God Army, the nation was at death's door.


As these grim events unfolded, Crown Princess Selvia of the Bariura Empire summoned her younger brother, Crown Prince Sirius, to the palace.
