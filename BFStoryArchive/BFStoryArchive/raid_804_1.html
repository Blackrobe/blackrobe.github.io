Voice
Hello, Hans.

Voice
Tell us, do you enjoy stories?

Voice
Ah, of course. You were expecting someone else. A familiar voice, perhaps.

Voice
Do not worry. Consider us allies of convenience--after all, you are in the right place at the right time. For now.

Voice
So, stories. Tragedy. Drama. Loss.

Voice
You believe these things to be merely transient feelings, emotions that flit from life to life.

Voice
But for us, Hans, who exist between the boundaries of the enfleshed and the immaterial, stories are...our existence.

Voice
Our reason for life. Without stories, 'we' would cease to exist.

Voice
So as we draw back the curtain in this tale of bloody woe, consider: whose story are you in? And who--or what--pulls _your_ strings?

Voice
Enjoy the show. 'We' certainly will.
