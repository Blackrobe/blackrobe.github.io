Several hours later, the Imperial Capital of Bariura fell to the God Army and the Bariura Empire vanished from Grand Gaia.


Third-born princess Berdette broke through the God Army's siege, leading the surviving citizens as she fled.The stories tell us that afterwards, she disguised herself as a commoner and joined a group on its way to a new promised land.


The Emperor of Bariura traveled to another world alongside Crown Prince Sirius, and their group challenged a divine beast to combat in order to secure power with which to challenge the God Army.


However, their attempt ended in failure. The Emperor of Bariura was gravely wounded, and did not survive.


Later, Sirius would elect to coexist with the divine beast in its world.The tales tell us that he was never able to make good on his promise to Myuze, the fourth-born princess of Bariura. Sirius would never again return to Grand Gaia.
